About us

A-RAY is a Student Sentiment analysis project based on data collected via the website. The project's purpose is to show a better understanding of students sentiments during E-learning.Students had underlying difficulties throughout the crisis, so we intend to examine their feelings from their perspectives. In this website, we will examine student's perception of their e-learning experiences and make recommendations for the future. This website also includes a general analysis report of the students's experiences and mental states during the e-learning process.

Good day, everyone! We are working on a project called A-RAY (Accumulated Report Analysis for Youth) to analyse student productivity and sentiments during the Covid-19 online education system. We need real-time data for our analysis, which is why we are collecting information from students. We ask that you all collaborate and fill out the form. We assure you that your data will only be used for research purposes, and we appreciate your cooperation. Please fill out the form by clicking on the link below.
